Teach English! It’s super fun!

Tsai Hsing School is an esteemed institution with a rich history in Taipei and a new kindergarten branch in Kunshan, China. Banyan Global Learning recruits and supports teachers in both Taiwan and China and offers distance learning to the elementary and junior high school in Taipei. Our English program combines content-based instruction, student-centered activities and traditional methods to promote academic and real-world usage of the English language.

If you are interested in applying, please email a cover letter and resume to info@banyangloballearning.com. See further information below.

Kindergarten English Homeroom Teacher

Kunshan (Shanghai), China - 1-year contract Compensation Monthly salary of 17,000 RMB. If a teacher stays for an optional second year, the monthly salary is 18,700 RMB.
